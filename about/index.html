About me

Hello, my name is Junjian (Joey) Chen, a passionate software developer currently pursuing my computer engineering Master’s degree at the University of Toronto in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. With a strong foundation in computer engineering from McGill University, I’ve had the opportunity to delve into various facets of technology, from backend to frontend development, AI, and data analytics.

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I’ve worked on a wide range of projects, including developing cloud-based applications, user-friendly web platforms and AI-driven solutions. My main skill sets include but not limit to: Full-Stack Java + Vue.js development, MERN stack development, database(MySQL, MongoDB, Redis, PostgreSQL) and cloud computing(AWS EC2, Azure Synapse Analytics, Alibaba Cloud VM).

Feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn or check out my projects on GitHub!
